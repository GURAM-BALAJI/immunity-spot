about us

Immunity Spot LLP is the largest manufacturer and exporter of a range of high quality One (Ready to Mix), Premixes, Vending Machines and a wide range of hot beverages. We continuously innovate to introduce products to match the changing demands of discerning customers. We are continuously growing to serve the much-loved taste of homemade tea and other immunity beverages.


We’re not just passionate purveyors of Coffee and Tea, but everything else that goes with a full and rewarding coffee machine experience. We also offer a selection of premium teas, health drinks and immunity drinks to please the taste buds. And the service you get from us is an appeal by itself.


From the day we started, Immunity Spot has developed products and served customers with evolving vision. When acted upon with passion and determination, these visions led us to achieve many milestones. As our Healthy journey continues, we keep reflecting on our journey proudly.


Everything we do here at Immunity Spot is driven by purpose and continuous need for innovation. We aspire to be the brand of choice for a wide range of beverages which make the customers lives easier and more Healthy. We are working towards establishing Immunity Spot as the first brand of choice for a wide range of customers with varying beverage preferences as we successfully serve them their choice of beverage day in and day out.


We are fully engaged, we connect with, laugh with, and uplift the moods of our customers – even if just for a few moments. Sure, it starts with the promise of a perfectly made beverage, but our work goes far beyond that. It’s really about connection of human and robotic artificial intelligents .
