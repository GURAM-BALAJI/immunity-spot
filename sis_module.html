One Of The Best Coffee House In Your Home Town

A shop-in-shop is exactly what the name suggests. It’s where IS Beverages Vending Machine installed in its own 2 * 2 Square Foot retail space inside the retailer’s store. This could be anything from a stand to a full-blown mini store branding. It’s also known store-within-a-store. For the retailer with his / her store, a shop-in-shop partner helps to cover running costs by paying rent for the space they occupy in the store. It’s a way for retailers to keep their store feeling fresh and to always have something new to offer customers without having to come up with concepts or assortments themselves. It can also help retailers in attracting more footfall, a wider customer base & make more Profits. In most cases, there is a certain level of cross-over between the customer base of the two brands involved in a shop-in-shop concept, but there is also a point of differentiation. It may be that the partner brand sells a product range that the host store doesn’t, or it is an industry leader that the host knows customers will be looking for. In this case, many would prefer to benefit to some degree from the sale by facilitating IS beverages through their store. The benefits for the retailer opening the smaller space inside existing space include lower running costs. It’s far cheaper to fit out and operate a small-scale space than an entire brand store. The host store may even cover the staffing of the partner space. In some cases, a shop in shop are attractive locations that they couldn’t afford to on their own.
